## Update: The outage was rescheduled to October 20, 2022 #

The outage planned to happen on Oct 13, was rescheduled to October 20.

## IMPORTANT NOTICE: MAINTENANCE OF POWER AND COOLING SYSTEMS - October 13, 2022 ##

   Starting Thursday, October 13th, 2022, at 9:00 AM (Winnipeg Time), the Grex
   cluster will be unavailable to all users as we perform cluster maintenance.
   All running jobs will be terminated. A reservation will be set to prevent
   longer from starting if they can not finish before the outage.

   The maintenance should be completed by the end of the day around 4:00 PM.

   To take advantage of the cluster for the period before the outage, please
   submit short jobs that should finish before the beginning of the outage.

   Start Time : 9 AM (Winnipeg Time), Thursday, October 13, 2022
   Anticipated End Time : 4 PM (Winnipeg Time), Thursday, October 13, 2022
