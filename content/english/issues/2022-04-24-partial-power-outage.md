## A series of brownouts

There were two brief power interruptions in Grex datacentre, one on Saturday, APril 24 and another on Sunday, April 24. Most of compute nodes got rebooted.
Most running jobs were lost. Overall, the Grex keept working, network, login nodes, storage and managing VMs seems to have been unaffected.
