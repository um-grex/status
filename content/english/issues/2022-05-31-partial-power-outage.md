## A brownout rebooted 130 compute nodes 

There was a  brief power interruptions in Grex datacentre. Many legacy of compute nodes got rebooted.
Most running jobs were lost. Overall, the Grex keept working, network, login nodes, storage and managing VMs seems to have been unaffected.
