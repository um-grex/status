## A series of brownouts

There were at least two brief power interruptions in Grex datacentre. Some, but not all of the compute and login nodes rebooted as a result. Some, but not all, running jobs were lost.
Overall, the Grex keept working, network, storage and managing VMs seems to have been unaffected.

If you have questions or concerns, please don’t hesitate to contact us at: support@computecanada.ca , mentioning Grex in the subject line.
